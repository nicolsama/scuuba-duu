~ Deepest Dives ~

    How to Play

    1. Control the diver with arrow keys & get points for diving deeper (↓)
    2. ← ↑ → ↓
    3. Collect coral and sand dollars for bonus points
    4. Maintain O2 levels by colliding with bubbles
    5. Jellyfish shock and paralyze you
    6. Stingrays suck up O2 if close by
    7. Shark attack = immediate death
