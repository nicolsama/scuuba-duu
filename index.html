Deepest Dives

    How to Play

    1. ← ↑ → ↓
    2. Control the diver with arrow keys
    3. Get points for diving deeper (↓)
    4. Collect coral and sand dollars for bonus points
    5. Maintain O2 levels by colliding with bubbles
    6. Jelly Fish shock and paralyze you
    7. Sting Rays suck up O2 if close by
    8. Shark attacks = Immediate Death
